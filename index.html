Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" link in the navigation bar and fill out the registration form. You can choose to register as a renter or landlord.

Is SichrSpace free to use?

Yes, SichrSpace is free for renters to browse listings. Landlords can list their properties for free, with optional premium features available.

How do I contact a landlord or renter?

Once you find a listing you're interested in, use our secure messaging system to connect with the landlord or renter.

Can I list multiple properties?

Yes, landlords can list multiple properties under a single account.
